Hearing Injuries

Tinnitus, sound sensitivity, and hearing changes can follow noise exposure, impact, or other workplace incidents.

Head and Cognitive Injuries

Concussions and other head injuries may affect memory, focus, balance, mood, and tolerance for light or noise.

Stress-Related Harm

Traumatic or unsafe working conditions can contribute to anxiety, sleep disruption, fatigue, and loss of confidence.

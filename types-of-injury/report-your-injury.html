Documentation

Submit a Story

Use clear records, dates, symptoms, and witness information to make an invisible injury easier to explain and verify.

Write down the incident

Record what happened, when it happened, where it happened, who was present, and what symptoms appeared afterward.

Seek medical evaluation

Ask qualified providers to assess symptoms and keep copies of visit summaries, referrals, test results, and recommendations.

Notify the proper channel

Follow your workplace reporting process and keep copies of messages, forms, claim numbers, and responses.

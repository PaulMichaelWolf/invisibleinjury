Documentation

There are issues that can alter your ability to function and could require a convalescing period. This website is not intended for medical emergencies or legal documentation support. Please handle documentation for Worker's Compensation (WC) cases elsewhere.

Community Support

The United States provides benefits through Federal Medical Leave Act (FMLA) and Americans with Disabilities (ADA) Act. However, not all issues are acknowledged making some incidents, invisible to protections.

Accountability

Systems should take difficult-to-measure symptoms seriously and respond with fairness. Burden of proof that is needed for the liability aspects of modern medicine system is missing in some cases. There needs to be resources for those that fall outside the existing support frameworks.

People experiencing invisible injuries may be asked to continue working without appropriate support.

Workplace injuries do not have to be visible to be real, let's talk about it and support one another

Documentation

There are issues that can alter your ability to function and could require a convalescing period. This website is not intended for medical emergencies or legal documentation support. Please handle documentation for Worker's Compensation (WC) cases elsewhere.

Community Support

Behind every instigating incident, there is a story. The United States provides benefits through Federal Medical Leave Act (FMLA) and Americans with Disabilities (ADA) Act. However, not all issues are acknowledged within private sector accomodations. This makes some incidents, invisible.

Accountability

Systems should take difficult-to-measure symptoms seriously and respond with fairness. Burden of proof for invisible incidents in a liability-informed medical system is, missing. This platform could aid in organizing for reform through political action or non-profit.

People experiencing invisible injuries may be asked to continue working without appropriate support.
